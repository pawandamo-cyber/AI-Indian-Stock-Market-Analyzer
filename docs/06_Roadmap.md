# Project Vision

Build a professional AI-powered Indian Stock Market Analysis platform that combines:

- Live Market Data
- Technical Analysis
- Artificial Intelligence
- Financial Analysis
- Market News
- Portfolio Management

The goal is to provide investors with intelligent, easy-to-understand stock insights.
